About the Conference

The 15th International Scientific Conference on Earth Observation Tools in Protected Areas (EOPA 2025) will be held online on November 15‚Äì16, 2025, organized by BOCHER Startup in collaboration with:

  • University Consortium of Small Island States (UCSIS)
  • University of the West Indies (UWI), University of the South Pacific (USP), University of Seychelles, Fiji National University (FNU)
  • EUCLID, CCREEE, Plastic-I, Anemo Robotics, SOS Carbon, Defiant Robotics, and other SIDS institutions

Objective: Build capacity in marine Earth Observation (EO) data for SIDS, LDCs, and African countries. Improve access to chlorophyll-a, sea-surface temperature (SST), radiance (MERIS/MODIS), and coastal altimetry (AltiCoRe/NERC) data.

Languages: English, French, Portuguese, and Spanish.

Conference Topics

  • Monitoring of algal biomass and harmful algal blooms
  • Coastal sediment movements
  • Location of fronts, eddies, and upwelling zones
  • Palaeoecological research in Ocean Parks
  • MPAs as resources for human health and well-being
  • Researcher-manager collaboration in MPAs
  • Endemic species in marine parks and conservation
  • MPA objectives and transformation
  • MPAs as instruments for sustainable regional development
  • Tourism, culture, and aesthetics in ocean parks
  • Climate change threats to endemic species
  • Wildlife management challenges in MPAs
  • MPA creation trajectories in SIDS
  • Environmental damages in SIDS MPAs
  • Glacial lakes in national parks (case studies)
  • Research interest in SIDS MPAs
  • Ecosystem services in Danube Delta
  • A multi-taxa approach for SIDS and LDCs

Proposals on related themes are welcome.
