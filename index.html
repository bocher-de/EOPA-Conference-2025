About the Conference

The 15th International Scientific Conference on Earth Observation Tools in Protected Areas (EOPA 2025) will be held online on November 15‚Äì16, 2025, organized by BOCHER Startup in collaboration with the University Consortium of Small Island States (UCSIS).

Objective: To build capacity and generate knowledge of marine Earth Observation (EO) data in Small Island Developing States (SIDS), improve access to chlorophyll-a, sea-surface temperature (SST), radiance (MERIS/MODIS), and coastal altimetry (AltiCoRe/NERC) data for African and LDC countries.

Languages: English, French, Portuguese, and Spanish.
